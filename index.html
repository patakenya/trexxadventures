Our Story

Founded in 2018, Trexx Adventures was born from a passion for sharing Kenya's natural wonders and cultural heritage with the world. Based near Tatu City, we've grown from a small team of enthusiastic guides to a premier adventure company.

Our mission is to create authentic, sustainable travel experiences that connect visitors with Kenya's landscapes, wildlife, and communities while preserving these treasures for future generations.
