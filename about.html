Our Story

Our Story

Founded in 2018, Trexx Adventures was born from a love for Kenya’s wild landscapes and rich cultures. Based near Tatu City, we set out to create authentic travel experiences that connect adventurers with the heart of Kenya. From the vast plains of Maasai Mara to the peaks of Mount Kenya, our journeys celebrate the country’s natural beauty and heritage.

What started as a small team of passionate guides has grown into a trusted name in Kenyan travel, offering safaris, hiking, cultural tours, and team-building adventures. We’re committed to crafting memories that last a lifetime.
